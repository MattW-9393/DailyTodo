User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Level Up system and get the most out of your experience.

Can I change the exercises?
The exercises shown in the Level Up system are indicative. Choose exercises you're confident doing in high volume.
Can I change the reps?
Currently, reps are fixed. You can modify them by editing the HTML source code.
Can I change the reps/exercises?
Customization is not yet supported, but may be added in future versions.
I have an issue with the app. Where do I report this?
Use the "I have an issue" link at the bottom of the home page. Be detailed when submitting an issue.
Is this a proper fitness plan?
No. This app is a habit tracker. Exercises are for illustration only. Please consult professionals before starting intense physical activity.
